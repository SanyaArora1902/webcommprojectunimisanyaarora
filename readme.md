## The Idea

Creating a complete and modern web presence for an Indian fine-dining restaurant located in Lodi, aimed at presenting: <br>
- The restaurant identity and atmosphere <br>
- A structured and elegant menu <br>
- Practical information (location, opening hours, contacts) <br>
- Online table reservation <br>
- A visual style inspired by high-end Italian restaurant websites <br>

___

## Concept and Visual Identity

The concept combines: <br>
- Royal Blue and Gold color palette inspired by Indian royal aesthetics <br>
- Elegant typography similar to Michelin-style Italian restaurants <br>
- Large food photography and minimal layout <br>

## Information Architecture

The website is structured into the following sections: <br>
- Home (hero, introduction, call to action) <br>
- Menu (categorised dishes with images and prices) <br>
- About (philosophy, experience, atmosphere) <br>
- Reservations (validated booking form) <br>
- Contact & Location (map, hours, newsletter form) <br>

## Technologies Used

The project was developed using: <br>
- HTML5 with semantic structure <br>
- CSS3 and Bootstrap 5 for layout and responsiveness <br>
- JavaScript for: <br>
  - Form validation <br>
  - Cookie consent banner <br>
  - Interactive elements (carousel, navigation) <br>
- Google Fonts (Playfair Display) <br>
